# AI Risk Understanding Assessment

A quiz that figures out what you know about AI safety and tells you where to go next.

Live: https://assessment-xi-hazel.vercel.app

## What it does

Starts with three questions about your background, familiarity with the field, and how much time you have. Then gives you a quiz -- 10, 15, or 25 questions depending on your answer -- across five domains:

- AI Risk Fundamentals
- Technical Concepts
- Governance and Policy
- Near vs. Long-term Risks
- What Can Be Done

At the end you get a knowledge profile broken down by domain, a reading list matched to your weak spots, and next steps based on who you are.

## Questions

25 questions written from scratch, scenario-based rather than trivia. Options are shuffled on every run. Each question shows the correct answer and an explanation after you answer.

## Running locally

No build step. Open `index.html` in a browser.

## Stack

Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript. No dependencies, no bundler. Single file.
